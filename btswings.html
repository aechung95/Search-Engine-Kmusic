BTS:Wings

Wings is the second studio album by boy band BTS. The album was released on October 10, 2016 by Big Hit Entertainment. It is available in four versions and contains fifteen tracks, with "Blood Sweat and Tears" as its lead single.

Wings is a unique album with a fascinating storyline about growth, using the symbol of wings to represent growth and coming-of-age. The album is also notable in that it includes the solo tracks of all 7 members, yet still links each track to the overall concept. The complex and puzzling imagery used in the album’s promotions and music videos left many fans in a daze, resulting in some interesting theories regarding the music video for “Blood Sweat and Tears,” as well as each member’s solo promotions.
